# Semester Project Brief

## Purpose

Each team will carry one industry context through the semester and analyze digital disruption within that context.

The project is cumulative. Weekend 1 builds diagnosis. Later weekends build strategic options, transformation logic, and governance reasoning.

## Project stages

### Stage 1. Industry diagnostic
Teams define the industry, map major actors, identify disruption forces, and structure the problem.

### Stage 2. Competitive analysis
Teams analyze the industry using:
- Porter's Five Forces,
- value chain analysis,
- jobs-to-be-done framing.

### Stage 3. Strategic options
Teams identify and compare candidate strategic responses.

### Stage 4. Transformation roadmap
Teams design a practical transformation path.

## What strong projects do

- define the client context clearly,
- use frameworks precisely,
- connect evidence to claims,
- make tradeoffs explicit,
- write in a professional executive style.
