# Industry Context Brief: Retail Banking in Georgia and the South Caucasus

## Why this context works

Retail banking is under pressure from digital-only banks, fintech entrants, AI-based credit tools, mobile-first customer expectations, and changing trust dynamics.

## Industry overview

This context includes consumer-facing banking services such as current accounts, payments, personal finance tools, lending, and digital banking channels.

## Visible disruption forces

- mobile-first banking experience,
- fintech unbundling,
- AI-driven scoring and decision support,
- lower tolerance for branch-heavy friction,
- embedded finance and platform competition.

## Focal strategic question

How should an incumbent retail bank in the region respond to platform-based fintech competition and changing customer expectations without destroying trust, economics, or regulatory credibility?
