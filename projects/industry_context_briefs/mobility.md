# Industry Context Brief: Logistics and Last-Mile Delivery

## Why this context works

Logistics and last-mile delivery sit at the intersection of platform coordination, route optimization, data, labor economics, and operational AI.

## Industry overview

This context includes courier networks, platform aggregators, parcel delivery systems, warehouse coordination, and urban delivery operations.

## Visible disruption forces

- route optimization AI,
- platform-based dispatch,
- e-commerce growth,
- demand for real-time visibility,
- automation horizon in fleet and fulfillment.

## Focal strategic question

How should an incumbent logistics or delivery player respond to platform coordination and AI-driven operational competition while protecting service reliability and unit economics?
