# Industry Context Brief: Omnichannel Retail

## Why this context works

Retail is under direct pressure from e-commerce, marketplaces, personalization, AI merchandising, supply chain digitization, and platform-mediated customer relationships.

## Industry overview

This context includes physical retail, digital commerce, fulfillment, loyalty systems, and marketing channels.

## Visible disruption forces

- platform marketplaces,
- AI-driven personalization,
- channel shift to digital commerce,
- data-driven inventory optimization,
- shrinking tolerance for friction.

## Focal strategic question

How should a retail incumbent redesign its model in response to platform competition, customer expectation shifts, and the economics of digital commerce?
