# Airbnb Case

## Industry
Hospitality and short-term accommodation

## Incumbent logic
Hotels operate asset-heavy businesses with high capital intensity, standardized service models, and physical capacity constraints.

## Airbnb's move
Airbnb enabled hosts to monetize spare capacity through a digital marketplace that connected travelers with distributed accommodation supply.

## Strategic questions

- How did Airbnb alter the supply side of the market?
- Why was asset-light scale so powerful?
- Which customer jobs did Airbnb perform better than hotels?
