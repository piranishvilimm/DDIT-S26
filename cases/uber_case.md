# Uber Case

## Industry
Urban transportation and ride-hailing

## Incumbent logic
Traditional taxi markets relied on licensed medallions, geographic controls, dispatch systems, and regulated supply.

## Uber's move
Uber used a mobile platform to connect riders and drivers, reduce matching friction, increase convenience, and introduce dynamic supply coordination.

## Strategic questions

- Did Uber enter through a new market or by directly attacking an existing one?
- How much of Uber's success came from technology versus regulatory arbitrage?
- Which parts of the incumbent model were most exposed?
