# Tesla Case

## Industry
Automotive and mobility

## Incumbent logic
Traditional carmakers rely on dealer networks, long product cycles, internal combustion expertise, and fragmented digital experience layers.

## Tesla's move
Tesla combined electric vehicles, software-defined product logic, direct-to-consumer sales, over-the-air updates, and integrated brand identity.

## Strategic questions

- Is Tesla best understood as disruptive innovation, architectural innovation, or category redefinition?
- Which incumbent assumptions did Tesla break first?
- How does software change the economics of product improvement?
