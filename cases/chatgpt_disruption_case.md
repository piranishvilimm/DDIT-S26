# ChatGPT and the Disruption of Knowledge Work

## Why this case matters

This case gives students a live example of digital disruption affecting knowledge-intensive work.

## Central tension

Generative AI tools can produce drafts, summaries, coding assistance, ideation support, and research acceleration. But their impact varies sharply across tasks, professions, and business models.

## Discussion questions

- Which forms of knowledge work are most exposed?
- Which jobs are supplemented rather than replaced?
- Where does competitive advantage shift when generative tools become widely available?
- What happens to professional differentiation when baseline production becomes cheaper?
