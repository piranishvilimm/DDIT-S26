# Summary: Yoo, Lyytinen, and Boland (2010)

## Why this summary exists

The original article is conceptually dense. This summary highlights the ideas that matter most for Weekend 1.

## Core claim

Digital innovation is different because digital artifacts are layered, reprogrammable, and generative.

## What this means

- innovation can occur in multiple layers,
- control over one layer may create strategic power,
- third parties can create unexpected extensions,
- industry evolution becomes less linear.
