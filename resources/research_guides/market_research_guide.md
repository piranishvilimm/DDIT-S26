# Market Research Guide

## Purpose

This guide helps teams gather usable evidence for industry and competitive analysis.

## Good source types

- regulator data
- government statistics
- company annual reports
- reputable industry reports
- academic work
- investor presentations, used carefully
- earnings calls, used carefully

## Look for

- market size,
- growth rate,
- customer behavior shifts,
- entrant activity,
- strategic moves by incumbents,
- evidence of digital adoption.
