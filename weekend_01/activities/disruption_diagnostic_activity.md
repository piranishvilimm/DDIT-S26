# Activity: Disruption Diagnostic

## Purpose

Teams apply Christensen's framework to their own client context.

## Output
Completed `disruption_diagnostic_v1.md`

## Process

1. Identify the main disruption force in the client industry.
2. Identify the entrant, technology, or shift.
3. Evaluate entry pattern.
4. Assess incumbent response.
5. Classify the shift.
6. Write strategic implications.
