# Activity: Competitive Landscape Workshop

## Purpose

Teams build the backbone of their industry analysis.

## Outputs
- `industry_map_v1.md`
- `competitive_landscape_v1.md`
- `competitive_landscape_matrix_v1.md`

## Process

1. Map the industry system.
2. Run Porter Five Forces.
3. Build a value chain view.
4. Apply jobs-to-be-done competition logic.
5. Compare major competitors.
6. Identify white space and vulnerability zones.
