# Activity: Team Formation and Working Agreement

## Purpose

This activity creates the social operating system for the semester project.

## Output
Completed `team_working_agreement.md`

## Steps

1. Confirm team membership.
2. Share relevant background briefly.
3. Agree on meeting cadence.
4. Agree on communication channels.
5. Define work-quality expectations.
6. Define conflict resolution steps.
7. Record commitments in the template.
