# Reading List

## Weekend 1 required reading

1. David Rogers, *The Digital Transformation Roadmap*, Introduction  
Focus: five domains and strategic readiness

2. Christensen, Raynor, and McDonald, *What Is Disruptive Innovation?*  
Focus: low-end disruption, new-market disruption, sustaining innovation

3. Assigned one-page client industry brief  
Focus: industry structure, visible disruption forces, focal strategic question

## Weekend 1 supporting academic reading

Yoo, Lyytinen, and Boland, *Digital Innovation Research in Information Systems*  
Focus: layered architecture, generativity, and distributed innovation

## Weekend 2 pre-reading assigned during Weekend 1 sprint plan

- Agrawal, Gans, and Goldfarb, *Power and Prediction*, Chapters 1 to 3
- Parker, Van Alstyne, and Choudary, *Platform Revolution*, Chapters 1 to 4
- Iansiti and Lakhani, *Competing in the Age of AI*, Part I

## Reading discipline

Do not read for summary only. Read for:
- frameworks,
- definitions,
- analytical distinctions,
- assumptions,
- implications for your client context.
