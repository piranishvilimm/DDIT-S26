# Jobs-to-Be-Done Competition Analysis

## Core idea

Customers do not buy products in the abstract. They hire solutions to get a job done.

A competitor is not only another firm in the same category. A competitor is any alternative that performs the job the customer needs done.

## Questions

- What is the core job the customer is hiring the product to do?
- What functional needs matter?
- What emotional or social needs matter?
- What alternatives already solve this job, even imperfectly?
